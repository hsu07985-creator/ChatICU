# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and inclusive collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in technical discussions.
- Focus feedback on code and behavior, not people.
- Share context and evidence when proposing changes.
- Accept and act on constructive feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Public disclosure of sensitive security information.
- Repeatedly dismissing review feedback without technical rationale.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact maintainers through internal project channels.
For security concerns, follow `SECURITY.md` and do not disclose publicly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this policy.
Repeated or severe violations may result in removal from collaboration spaces.
